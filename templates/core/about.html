Academic FAQ

Are the courses recorded or live?

Our courses are premium recorded masterclasses that you can watch anytime. However, we also hold regular live Q&A sessions for all enrolled students.

Can I switch between courses?

Each course is a separate purchase for lifetime access, but if you buy a bundle, you get access to all included courses immediately.

What software do I need to install?

For Video Editing, we use Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects. For Photoshop, you'll need Adobe Photoshop (latest version recommended).
